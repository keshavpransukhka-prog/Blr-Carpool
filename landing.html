The basics

What is carpooling?

Carpooling means sharing a cab with strangers going the same direction — splitting the cost equally. A cab from BLR to Indiranagar costs ₹800–1200. Split two ways, that's ₹400–600 each.

💸

Save money

Split the fare equally with your co-passenger. The more people, the cheaper it gets.

🌱

Better for everyone

Fewer cabs on the road means less traffic and a smaller carbon footprint.

🤝

Meet fellow travellers

Most BLR arrivals are professionals and frequent flyers — good company for the ride.

Step by step

How does BLR Carpool work?

The whole process takes under 2 minutes. Here's exactly what happens.

1

Fill in your details

Enter your name, phone number, arrival time, and destination area. Your phone number is kept private until you match.

2

See who's arriving near you

We show you other passengers arriving within 1 hour of you, going to the same part of the city.

3

Send a carpool request

Tap "Request Carpool" on someone you'd like to share with. They'll see your request on their screen.

4

Wait for them to accept

If they also request you back, it's a mutual match. You'll both get a browser notification instantly.

5

Connect on WhatsApp

Once matched, both phone numbers are revealed and a WhatsApp button appears to message them directly.

Timing

When should you fill this?

BLR Carpool works best when used at the right moment. Here's our recommendation.

🛬

Best time: Right after you land

Open the app as soon as your flight touches down — while waiting for your bags at the carousel. This gives you 10–15 minutes to find a match before heading to the cab queue.

📅

Can I fill it before landing?

Yes! You can enter your scheduled arrival time in advance. We'll match you with others arriving within 1 hour of that time. Just make sure to check back after landing for any new matches.

Notifications

How will I know when I'm matched?

You don't need to keep staring at the screen. Here's how we notify you.

🔔

Browser notification

When you submit your details, we ask permission to send you notifications. The moment someone matches with you, a notification pops up on your phone — even if the app is in the background.

💬

WhatsApp button appears

When you open the app after a match, a green "Message on WhatsApp" button appears on your match's card. Tap it to instantly start a conversation with a pre-filled message.

After matching

What do I do after matching?

Once you've matched, here's the recommended flow to actually share the cab.

💬

Message on WhatsApp

Tap the green button to send a pre-filled message introducing yourself.

📍

Agree on a meeting point

Pick a clear spot — the Arrivals exit, a specific gate, or the prepaid taxi counter.

🪪

Exchange IDs

Show each other a photo ID to confirm you're the right person before getting in.

🚕

Book the cab together

Use Uber, Ola, or the prepaid taxi counter. Split the fare equally at the end.

Safety first

How to stay safe

We take safety seriously. Here are our recommendations before sharing a cab with someone you just met.

Meet in publicAlways meet your match in the public Arrivals area, not in a secluded spot.

Check their IDAsk to see an Aadhar card, passport, or driver's licence before sharing.

Share your locationDrop a live location to a trusted contact before getting in the cab.

Trust your instinctsIf something feels off, it's completely fine to decline and book separately.

Use official cabs onlyOnly use Uber, Ola, or the airport's official prepaid taxi counter.

Agree on fare upfrontConfirm how you'll split the fare before the ride starts to avoid confusion.
